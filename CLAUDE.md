## Operating rules established / reinforced (2026-06-27/28)
1. LIVE GMAIL IS THE SOURCE OF TRUTH. The board's `email_thread` is a cached scrape that lags real Gmail by weeks. Never trust a card's "needs reply" / stage without checking live Gmail. Verified repeatedly: cards showed mid-deal when the real conversation was delivered/paid/closed.
2. route_to_robert / human_only lane. Not every inbound is a Deal Desk sponsorship. Interviews, podcasts, intros, personal/relationship threads, and anyone who explicitly declines paid work are conversations for Robert to handle himself. These get flagged `draft_reply_status = "human_only"`, are never auto-drafted, and stay out of the reply/approval queue. When unsure between a deal and a Robert conversation, lean human_only.
3. Report before overwrite. Bulk board changes (clearing drafts, re-staging) are snapshotted first and reversible. Nothing sends without approval.
4. The "Where this stands" timeline only reflects real time-in-current-stage. It no longer fabricates a journey (see fix below).
